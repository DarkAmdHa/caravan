WHO
WE ARE.

Behind every great piece of content is a great idea. But between the concept and the final edit, the idea itself is lost. Why? Because those in production don’t really understand the concept.

Most production companies are just that: production companies. You give them the brief; they execute it. Yet without understanding the creative inspiration behind the brief, your vision is at risk. Your idea ends up being unprotected on set. Your asset output doesn’t get maximised. And you end up without a creative partner.

We’re more than just producers: we’re partners. We approach your project conceptually, actively working to understand the idea behind your vision - rather than passively implementing it. Which means that we anticipate problems before they arise. It means that we’re much better placed to find the best way of realising your idea. And it means that you can rely on us to turn your creative vision into a reality - even if it’s still just a rough sketch.

So that your big idea stays that way, no matter how small the screen.

WHAT WE DO

CREATION + STRATEGY

We work both with agencies and directly with brands

We work both with agencies and directly with brands themselves. With our strong creative and strategic team and a roster of directors that understand storytelling, we are equipped for both.

Whether you’re an agency or a brand working independently, we see ourselves as your partners. We approach content conceptually, collaborating with you to define the underlying idea driving your brand or campaign, and then ensuring that it is elaborated throughout your content clearly, consistently, and creatively.

Even if your content is in need of conceptual thinking, we can help there too. From website building to the final edit of the hero video promoting it, we have the designers, directors, and writers to realise your vision.

Supported by data and driven by narrative, we develop content with engagement at the core. Everything we create is maximised through our integrated production model. Want a 30 second manifesto film? We’ll show you how this single asset can be shot in a way that enables it to be amplified, from a YouTube pre-roll to key visuals and social media reels - all without breaking the bank (or your finance director’s heart).

Which means that you get the maximum output of assets in the most efficient and cost effective way possible.

PITCH SUPPORT

Pitching for that one big campaign?

Pitching for that one big campaign? We’ll help you with creatives, moods, location images, budget drafts, casting suggestions - whatever it takes to bring that project through your door.

We love creating decks: our in-house designers bring your ideas to life. And because we’re veterans of the pitching process, we’re by your side every step of the way, from pulling all-nighters to hitting those last-minute deadlines.

BUDGETING

There’s nothing sexier than a well-drawn budget

There’s nothing sexier than a well-drawn budget. Send over your idea, pitch deck or just drop us a few lines. We can get you a non-binding estimate within 12-24hs, helping to anticipate problems and ensuring that the creative and the budget are aligned.

And unlike other production companies, we use our expertise to give you a realistic understanding of what realising your content will actually cost. So that you’re equipped with the right information to make creative choices - no nonsense, no nasty surprises.

PRODUCTION

We help brands tell their stories

Narrative-led

We help brands tell their stories with digital content that engages, informs and activates audiences. And to do this, your content must tell an emotive story.

We understand the power of narrative. Even bitesize content can be made more compelling through the principles of great storytelling, earning clicks, views, and trust.

Globally experienced

For more than 10 years CARAVAN has been producing full service productions across the globe, giving us invaluable insights on everything from location costs to permits.

Asset-maximising

Our integrated production model helps you amplify your content across a whole campaign. Maximum output, minimal costs, media-agnostic.

POST PRODUCTION

Gunshots, splashes, explosions

Together with our post production partner, we give you access to state-of-the-art edit suites.

Gunshots, splashes, explosions: sound effects make stories burst into action. Our sound edit suite is a 100m2 studio with a 5.1 Protools 21 ultimate with dynaudio core 59 speakers and a full HD projector. And it includes a foley stage with concrete, wood or gravel pits and a special pit for water SFX - giving your content the amplification it needs to get your audience off their feet.

LOCATIONS + STUDIO

Our secret weapon

Our studio is 4764 square metres - but let’s put that in perspective. That’s the equivalent of 12 IMAX cinemas, 9 basketball courts, 3 Fort Knox’s - or just one of Bill Gates’s homes. We’re based in Tirana, Albania, our studio is the largest in the country, and stocked with industry-standard tools at industry-beating prices.

Wait - Albani-where? Most people have never been to Albania, let alone produced best-in-class content there. And before we established our studio here, we hadn’t either.

But we’re really glad we did. Because Albania is an outstanding set location.

Costs are in the thousands, not millions. Permits are obtained in days, not years. And the weather is balmy, not Baltic.

Albania is also blessed with diverse landscapes, from the Mediterranean coastline to the Alpine-esque mountains of its rugged interior.
